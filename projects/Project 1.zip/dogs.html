My dogs names are Daisy and Poppy, both named after flowers.

Daisy is the white one with the beard and she is around 4-5 years old. She's very nervous and has a lot of anxiety so she shakes a lot. She's very scared around other dogs, except for Poppy.

Poppy is just over year old and we got her over the summer. We decided to rescue instead of getting them from the pound or a breeder because there are so many dogs in the world looking for a home.
